Compressed Air Tank Caddy

Engineering Design Project with Interactive 3D Visualization

Project Overview

This project presents the engineering design of a compressed air tank caddy with capacity for 15 tanks in a 3×5 configuration. The design features proper edge reinforcement, structural support beams, and optimized tank spacing to ensure stability and usability.

Compressed Air Tank Caddy Design

Key Features

Optimized Configuration

Designed to hold 15 compressed air tanks in a 3×5 configuration with precise spacing for stability and accessibility.

Structural Integrity

Features 2" square steel tubing frame with proper edge reinforcement and critical structural support beams.

Heavy-Duty Construction

Built with 1/2" steel plate platform, 1/4" reinforcement rings, and 5" heavy-duty casters with 250lb capacity each.

Maximum Load Capacity

Engineered to safely handle up to 800lbs total weight with proper weight distribution.

Precise Dimensions

Overall dimensions: 39¼" width × 24½" length × 19¼" height, with 5⅝" diameter tank holes.
